Pizza

A dish of Italian origin consisting of a flattened disk of bread dough topped with some combination of olive oil, oregano, tomato, olives, mozzarella or other cheese, and many other ingredients.

It is baked quickly usually, in a commercial setting, using a wood-fired oven heated to a very high temperature and served hot.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Cheese
  • Tomatoes
  • Yeast
  • Olive oil

Steps

  1. Prepare ingredients
  2. Prepare dough
  3. Add toppings
  4. Bake the pizza
